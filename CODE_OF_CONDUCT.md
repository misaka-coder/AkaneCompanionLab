# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, specific, and constructive.

- Discuss technical decisions without attacking people.
- Assume good intent, but accept clear corrections when behavior causes harm.
- Do not publish private conversations, credentials, personal identifiers, or
  user data.
- Respect artists, authors, upstream projects, and their licensing terms.
- Do not pressure maintainers or contributors to provide unpaid support.
- Harassment, discrimination, threats, and deliberate disruption are not
  acceptable.

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content and restrict
participation when necessary to protect contributors and users.
